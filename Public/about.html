OUR STORY

WFLK The Flock is a fan-made parody radio station inspired by the classic vibes of Stranger Things.

Due to intellectual property considerations, we've rebranded from our original concept, but the magic remains the same. We're keeping the spirit of classic 80s radio alive with immersive DJ breaks, period-authentic commercials, and that nostalgic feeling that defined a generation.

Join The Flock as we broadcast non-stop hits from the golden era, complete with Easter eggs, hidden references, and the authentic radio experience you crave.

"The signal never dies. The Flock flies on."
